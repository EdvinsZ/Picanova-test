RULES OF THE GAME:

The cards are shown with the face down and are shuffled randomly

  1. There is 16 cards from which 8 of them unique

  2. Click on a card to turn it and see the image on the card.

  3. Then click on the second card to turn also the second card.

  4. If both cards show the same image, you get a point.

  5. If cards does not match, they flip back with face down.

  6. Find all pairs to win a game before time ends

  7. Less flips = better score
